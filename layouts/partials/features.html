Real Time Currency Ratio 💰

Users can enter a From Currency and To Currency of interest, and once they hit submit, the real time conversion ratio will be displayed.

Price Range of the Product of Interest

📈

We output the range of prices (min to max) on a given page in the new currency.

In-place New Currency Value 🛡️

Users can highlight a numerical price, and above the original price a new popup box will come up with the new converted price in the new currency.
